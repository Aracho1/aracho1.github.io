hello, i'm Ara

aspiring web developer

Hi, I am

from Seoul, Korea. When I turned 15, my family moved to Hanoi, Vietnam where I spent my teenage years in an international school making friends from all over the world. After graduating high school, I came to London for my studies and I was immediately drawn to the city's diverse and lively culture. I have been working as a business manager in a global bank in the last 5 years.

About a year ago, I started coding in the evenings and weekends for fun, with the help of my programmer boyfriend. Programming has become an increasingly important space for me to learn and be creative. I love that coding trains you to be both creative and logical.

When I'm not working or coding, I love cooking vegan food, practising yoga and running in the parks.

why I want to be a programmer

constant learning

I am someone who thrives on being exposed new challenges and learning new things. I love that programming challenges me to learn new concepts and codes everyday. I know that becoming a programmer is a journey of constantly learning new codes, concepts and technology, which excites me.

problem solving

I love the exhilarating moment when I finally figure out how to solve a problem I have been working on for a long time. The process can be slow and frustrating but persistence and hard work often leads to a result.

making an impact

What I truly love about programming is that I can see something I created come to life. It is an empowering skill and feeling to see a code changing things even if it is something small. I would love to build or contribute something through programming that makes a positive impact in people's lives.
