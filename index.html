hello, i'm Ara

aspiring web developer

Hi, I am

from Seoul, Korea. When I turned 15, my family moved to Hanoi, Vietnam where I spent my teenage years in an international school making friends from all over the world. After graduating high school, I came to London for my studies and I was immediately drawn to the city's diverse and lively culture. I have been working as a business manager in a global bank in the last 5 years.

About a year ago, I started coding in the evenings and weekends for fun, with the help of my programmer boyfriend. Programming has become an increasingly important space for me to learn and be creative. I love that coding trains you to be both creative and logical.

When I'm not working or coding, I love cooking vegan food, practising yoga and running in the parks.
